Mapping FGM in Nigeria

The Nigeria Demographic and Health Survey records FGM practices according to states. The prevalence of FGM is placed on a five-point scale that ranges from very low to very high.

  1. Very Low. Up to 2.8 percent of women have undergone any form of FGM.
  2. Low. Between 2.9 and 11 percent of women have undergone any form of FGM.
  3. Medium. Between 11.2 and 31.9 percent of women have undergone any form of FGM. The three most prevalent states in this category are Abia, Kaduna and Anambra.
  4. High. Between 32.2 and 45 percent of women have undergone any form of FGM. The three most prevalent states in this category are Ondo, Edo and Kano.
  5. Very High. Up to 76.6 percent of women have undergone any form of FGM. The states with the highest prevalence are Osun, Ebonyi and Ekiti.
